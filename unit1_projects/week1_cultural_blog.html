Kia toa, kia toa.

I am nervous. I am excited. I am nervous.
I know my life is about to change with DBC.
My nerves are calmed a bit when I realize
there are so many people willing to go through
this crazy experience with me.

So to my fellow cohorts, thank you.

The experience has been fairly enjoyable thus far...
but to be honest, it feels like I am holding my breath,
anticipating the part where I get kicked in the brain:

The late nights where I feel hopeless struggling through a tough problem.
Feeling less than confident when pairing with someone smarter.
Wondering if I'm cut out for this and if it's all worth it.

These are very vulnerable feelings.
But recent impressions of DBC have made me realize the importance
of vulnerability in a program that will test us mentally,
emotionally, and psychologically.

I look forward to being immersed in the DBC culture
while becoming a better programmer and a better person.
